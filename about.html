About Parker Zotz

I grew up in the heart of Windthorst, TX, surrounded by the dairy farms and cattle ranches that power our community. I know firsthand that when equipment breaks or a fence line fails, you don’t have time to wait around for a high-priced corporate crew.


With a background running custom harvesting jobs, operating heavy machinery, and supporting oil field infrastructure, I’ve learned how to weld for durability and precision. Whether it’s custom-building a specialty piece of equipment or repairing a gate so it lasts another twenty years, I’m here to make sure your operation stays running.


My goal is simple: to provide North Texas farmers and ranchers with affordable, high-quality welding services.
