About Parker Zotz

I grew up in the heart of Windthorst, TX, surrounded by the dairy farms and ranches that power our community. I know firsthand that when equipment breaks or a fence line fails, you don’t have time to wait around for a high-priced corporate crew.

With a background running harvesting equipment and working the demanding pace of the oil rigs, I’ve learned how to weld for durability and precision. My goal is simple: to provide North Texas farmers and ranchers with affordable, high-quality welding services.

Whether it’s custom-building a specialty piece of equipment or repairing a gate so it lasts another twenty years, I’m here to make sure your operation stays running.
